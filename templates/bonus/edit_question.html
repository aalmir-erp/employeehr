Guidelines

Good Questions:

  • Are clear and specific
  • Measure observable behaviors
  • Focus on a single aspect of performance
  • Use objective criteria

Examples:

  • Rate the employee's ability to meet deadlines
  • How well does the employee communicate with team members?
  • Rate the quality of work produced during the evaluation period
