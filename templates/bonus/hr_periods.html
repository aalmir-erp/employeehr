About Evaluation Periods

Evaluation periods are specific timeframes (monthly, quarterly, or annual) during which employee performance is assessed for bonus calculations. Each period follows this workflow:

1. Open

Period is active and supervisors can submit evaluations for their department employees.

2. In Review

HR is reviewing department submissions. No new submissions accepted.

3. Approved

All evaluations are approved and ready for processing in Odoo.

4. Closed

Period is complete and archived. Bonus amounts have been processed.

How the Bonus Evaluation Process Works

For HR Administrators:
  1. Create an Evaluation Period
    Define a specific timeframe (month, quarter, year) for evaluations.
  2. Define Evaluation Questions
    Create department-specific assessment criteria with weighted values.
  3. Review Submissions
    Approve or reject supervisor evaluations with feedback.
  4. Process Final Bonuses
    Export approved evaluations to Odoo for payroll processing.
For Department Supervisors:
  1. Access Active Period
    View current evaluation periods from the supervisor dashboard.
  2. Evaluate Department Employees
    Rate employees using department-specific criteria and scoring ranges.
  3. Submit for Approval
    Send completed evaluations to HR for review and processing.
  4. Address Feedback
    Revise rejected evaluations based on HR feedback.
Pro Tip: Create evaluation periods ahead of time to allow supervisors to plan their assessments. A typical schedule is monthly or quarterly evaluations with one comprehensive annual review.
